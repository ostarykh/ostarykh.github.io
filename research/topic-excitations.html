Elementary excitations of frustrated magnets

  • Elementary excitations above magnetically ordered state: spin waves (magnons).

  • Elementary excitations of critical Heisenberg chain: spin-1/2 spinons (domain walls). But they can not propagate between chains individually.

  • Elementary excitations of spin chains coupled by frustrated inter-chain exchange: S=1 triplons (bound states of two spinons; no rotational symmetry breaking). Spinons and triplons in spatially anisotropic frustrated antiferromagnets , M. Kohno, O.A. Starykh and L. Balents, Nature Physics 3 , 790-795 (2007).
  • Important technical details are in Supplementary material, and arXiv:0706.2012
