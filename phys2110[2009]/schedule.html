  • Homeworks are due at 4 pm on the day indicated in the Schedule below. Every homework problem is worth 10 points. Every graded homework is assigned numerical grade = (number of points given)/(total number of points). Cumulative grade for the homework is calculated at the end of the semester as follows:
    A for 85-100 % ; B for 65-85 % ; C for 50-65 % ; D for 30-50 %; F for less than 30 %. Two lowest-score homeworks will be droped from the calculation.
