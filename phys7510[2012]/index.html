The main purpose of the class is to provide an accessible introduction to revolutionary developments in condensed matter physics that took place in last few years. The key topic to be covered is physics of graphene and closely related to it topological insulators and their various interesting properties.

Book info: textbook "A quantum approach to condensed matter physics" by P. L. Taylor and O. Heinonen should be on reserve in the Marriott Library. Just ask for the book for Phys7510.
