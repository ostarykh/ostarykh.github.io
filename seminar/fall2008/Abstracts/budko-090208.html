Condensed Matter Seminar

Sergey Bud'ko
Ames Lab

Tuesday, September 2, 2008; 4:00 pm in JFB 334

Pnictide superconductors: brief introduction and experimental update

I will present brief overview of the experimental results on novel pnictide superconductors and their parent compounds: oxypnictides and AFe2As2 (A = Ca, Sr, Ba) materials. In the second part of the talk pressure induced superconductivity and P-T phase diagrams in AFe2As2 will be discussed.
