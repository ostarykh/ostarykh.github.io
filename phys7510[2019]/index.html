Course Description [3 credit hours]

The main purpose of the class is to provide an accessible introduction to revolutionary developments in condensed matter physics that took place in recent years. The key topic to be covered is the physics of graphene and closely related to it topological insulators and superconductors, and their various interesting properties.

 

The course assumes knowledge of basic Solid State Physics, on the level of Phys 5510 (Solid State Physics I) which is a pre-requisite for this class.
