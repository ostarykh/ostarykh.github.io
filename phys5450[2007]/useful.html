Useful Info and Link

Few other texts on quantum mechanics:

  • A Modern Approach to Quantum Mechanics, John S. Townsend, University Science Books, 2000; ISBN 1891389130 .
  • The Feynman Lectures on Physics, vol.3, Quantum Mechanics, Addison Wesley Longman (1970); ISBN 0201021153 . The classic and inspiring reading. Townsend's book is largely motivated by Feynman's.
  • Modern Quantum Mechanics, J.J. Sakurai, Addison Wesley Longman (1994); ISBN 0-201-53929-2 (this book is often used in graduate level quantum courses Phycs 7220 and 7230).
  • Quantum Mechanics (non-relativistic theory), L.D. Landau and E.M. Lifshitz, Butterworth-Heinemann, 3rd edition (1981); ISBN 0750635398 . A must for advanced theory students.
